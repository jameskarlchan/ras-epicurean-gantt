Connect to Google Sheets

Make your Google Sheet the permanent home for your data. Edit there, regenerate here.

STEP 1 — Get your starter data.
Click ⬇ CSV for Sheets in the toolbar to download the current task list.
STEP 2 — Create a Google Sheet.
Go to sheets.google.com → blank sheet → File → Import → Upload → drop the CSV in → Replace spreadsheet. Name it "Epicurean 2026 Master" or similar.
STEP 3 — Publish the sheet as CSV.
In the sheet: File → Share → Publish to web. Set "Entire document" dropdown to the tab you want, set the format to Comma-separated values (.csv), click Publish. Copy the URL it gives you.
STEP 4 — Paste it below.
Paste your published CSV URL here. Click Connect. From now on, "Generate" pulls fresh data from your sheet.
IMPORTANT. Once connected, edits in this tool are temporary — every Generate overwrites them with what's in the sheet. Edit in Sheets, regenerate here. The sheet is the master.
